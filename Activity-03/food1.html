Sweet and Sour Pork

  1. For Deep Frying
    • 600ml vegetable oil, for a 6.5 inch saucepan we used 600ml of oil, alternatively use a smaller saucepan. You want about an inch of oil in the bottom of the pan.
  1. For The Pork
    • 200g pork shoulder, cut into 1 inch cubes
    • 1/4 tsp Salt
    • White Pepper, to taste
    • 1/2 egg, beaten
    • 40g potato starch or cornflour (either works fine)
  1. For The Sauce
    • 35g vinegar
    • 55g granulated sugar
    • 25g ketchup
    • 5g tomato puree
    • 10g Worcester sauce
    • 1 tsp potato starch or cornflour (either works fine), to add into sauce before you cook with it

      • Note: The sauce will be red but not bright red like the takeaways, as they will probably use food colouring to get that colour.

  1. For The Dish
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 1 pineapple ring
    • 1/2 small onion, cut into chunks
    • 1/4 red pepper, cut into chunks
    • 1/4 green pepper, cut into chunks

Preparation

Step 1: Heat the oil in the pan on medium-high heat whilst you prepare everything else.

Step 2: Prepare the vegetables for the dish.

Step 3: Prepare the pork. Cut into 1 inch cubes. Season the meat with salt and sprinkle of white pepper. Pour half a beaten egg in and coat the pork pieces. Then mix in the potato starch (or cornflour) and coat the pieces again.

Cooking Steps

Step 1: To make the sweet and sour sauce: In a saucepan pour the vinegar and add the granulated sugar in, heat these up on a low heat until the sugar dissolves.

Step 2: Add the ketchup, tomato puree and Worcester sauce and mix. Put this to one side as the sauce is now complete and ready.

Step 3: Turn the oil to low heat. Take your chicken pieces and place them individually into the oil. Move them around, with a wooden spatula or wooden chopsticks, so they don't stick to each other. Fry them for 3 minutes.

Step 4: When the pork pieces start to float, turn the heat to high and fry for an extra 1 minute. Use a slotted spoon and take the pieces out onto a plate and leave on the side for now.

Step 5: To put the dish together. Heat wok on high heat. Add 1 tbsp vegetable oil.

Step 6: Add in peppers , onion and pineapple and fry for 1 minute.

Step 7: Take the sauce and add in 1 tsp of potato starch (or cornflour) and mix in. Add sauce to the pan and cook for 1 minute.

Step 8: Add pork pieces to the pan and stir everything together. Once everything is coated with sauce, it is ready to serve.

Done! Enjoy your meal :)
