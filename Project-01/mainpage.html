Where is Hong Kong?

Introduction

Hong Kong is a territory located on China's southern coast. It consists of the island of Hong Kong and adjacent islets in the South China Sea, the Kowloon Peninsula, and the New Territories. The entire territory was returned to China in 19971. Hong Kong is one of Asia's most vibrant and diverse metropolises, blending traditional culture with modern development. The city is situated at the southern tip of the Pearl River Delta and is known for its diverse cuisine, architecture, and festive culture.
